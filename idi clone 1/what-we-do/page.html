What We Do

From ocean to your door

Strategically Located

Our main processing plant in Honolulu is located at the Domestic Commercial Fishing Village at Pier 38, only a few minutes from the Honolulu airport. Many of our premium seafood products are delivered straight from the Honolulu Auction block.

Premium Products

selecting the highest quality products

Our seasoned buyers select only the highest quality seafood that are then delivered to our skilled team of processors and grading technicians. By ensuring all aspects, from auction selection to packaging and delivery, is met with care and integrity, we’re able to consistently deliver high quality seafood straight to your door.

Daily Deliveries

Our Oahu fleet of refrigerated vehicles are able to deliver to all restaurants, hotels, and markets.

Competitive Pricing

An efficient process of sourcing and processing, partnered with our comprehensive staff, allows FIF to provide our customers with competitive prices without sacrificing quality.

Exporting

Through our strong relationships with trade partners and our experienced team, Fresh Island Fish has become the leading seafood supplier for businesses around the Pacific Rim.We have a wide network of national and international customers who rely on our ability to deliver the freshest products directly from our Honolulu facility.
